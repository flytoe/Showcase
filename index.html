Process and Skills

Conception and Prototyping

After analysing the problem and emphasising with the end user-persona, I focus on the user's needs and requirements to aim the most comfortable way of using a product or service. A solid information architecture and conception in form of flowcharts and wireframes enable me to create early prototypes of the story the product is telling.

I iterate the prototypes to shape the product for a successful launch. I love, exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff to improove and redefine current mindsets to create joyfull and successful user experiences with meaningful user interfaces.

UI/UX and Graphic Design

A solid concept is the base for the user interface. It defines how a hardware or software product looks, feels and behaves to the users input.

About me

As an interactiondesigner I create products and services for users and their needs. Through innovative concepts, rapid prototyping and elaborated design solutions I shape the overall User-Experience (UX) and define the most comfortable friendliest way of using the product.

For the best team performance and the fasted way to create products, I work with two friends at threethink.io, because workflows are already setup.

I life in Germany and graduated from HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd with a Bachelor of Arts in Interaction Design in 2013. Additionally and studied Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand in 2011.

Conception and Prototyping

Conception and Prototyping

Creating solid concepts throughUser research, wireframing-and rapid prototyping techniques is just the first part of the journey. To drive innovation, never stop exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff. There is always place to redefine current mindsets to create joyfull and successful user experiences. Prototyping in early stages of the design process make you fail early and give you the possibilities to shape the product for a successful launch of the product.

User Interface Design

User Interface Design

The second part, the User Interface Design (UI-Design) and the right story telling shape the overall User-Experience (UX) and define the most comfortable way of experiencing a product or service. User Inferace Design is about the interaction between a user and a product. UI-Design defines how a hardware or software product looks, behaves, feels and responds to a user. It can be a screen related product, for example Software Applications or Web Design. But can also be hardware related, for example how the buttons in your kitchen appliances feel and respond to you.

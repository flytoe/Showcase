Hello, I'm a
UI/UX-Designer, Prototyper, Thinker, Problem-Solver, Concepter, Supporter, Enthusiast, Explorer, User


I am Patric Sterrantino, a freelancer for interactiondesign located in the innovative city Stuttgart in Germany. I love solving complex problems and guide you through a user centered design process. I have expertise in UX-Conception, Prototyping & UI-Design for mobile Apps, Web-Services, Software and Embedded Systems.

I have experience in automotive, life science, connected living and manufacturing industry.

I simplify processes and put myself in the user's position to understand what the user needs to succeed. Wireframes and interactive prototypes enable me to explore in early stages to shape the story the product is telling.

"I love, exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff. So I can improve and redefine current mindsets to create more joyful and successful user experiences. I put meaning to the user interface of products and services according to the users needs."

I define how a hardware or software product looks, feels and behaves to the users input, responding on the users context and needs. From low-tech wearables over Mobile Devices and Desktop-Software to high-end Industry Machinery and Embedded Devices.

With this excerpt of some of my recent works I want to give you a rough idea of what I am doing. Some of my work is already public, some will never be public and some was just for fun … You can even find more porjects on my tumblr.

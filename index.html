About me

As an Interaction-Designer I create products and services for users and their needs. Through innovative conception techniques, rapid prototyping and elaborated Interface-Design solutions I shape the overall User-Experience (UX) and define the most comfortable way of using the product.

For the best team performance and the fasted way to create products, I work with two friends at threethink.io, because workflows are already setup.

I life in Germany and graduated from HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd with a Bachelor of Arts in Interaction Design in 2013. In 2011 I studied Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand.

Customers I worked for

BOSCH moovel Car2Go Black Walter AG TDM Systems BSH Zeiss Vision Zeiss Meditec

Show work #2

Prototyper

Prototyper is a desktop application which allows you to make your ideas tangible. It offers options you just know from overloaded design- and complex animation-software. Prototyper unifies the worlds of animation, designing and interaction in one perfectly suited tool. Suddenly you have new opportunities to experience your ideas in early planing stages during the design process. With Prototyper you can design pixel-perfect and remain flexible at all times. You can have working prototypes within minutes, without having to translate your designs into code. With Prototyper you can explore interactions with graphical user interfaces more quickly and develop and test it faster. So you have more time to plan the next big thing.

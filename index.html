Conception and Prototyping

I focus on the user's needs and requirements to aim the most comfortable way of using a product or service. A solid information architecture and conception in flowcharts and wireframingenable me to create early prototypes of the story the product is telling. I iterate the prototypes to shape the product for a successful launch. I love, exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff to improove and redefine current mindsets to create joyfull and successful user experiences with meaningful user interfaces.

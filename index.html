About me

As an Interaction-Designer I create products and services for users and their needs. Through innovative conception, rapid prototyping techniques and elaborated User-Interface (UI) solutions I shape the overall User-Experience (UX) and define the most comfortable way of using the product. This amplifyies human capibilities by offering the right tool at the right time.

On this website you can find some excerpts of my Work for your inspiration. If you want to start a project with me, let me know! Two friends and I founded threethink for more complex projects. We are two designers and one developer, offering a fast project development, because workflows are already setup for the best teamperformance.

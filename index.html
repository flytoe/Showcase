What I usually do

First of all I analyse the problem to solve and emphatize with the end user-persona. Next I focus on the user's needs and requirements to aim the most comfortable way of using a product or service. Wireframes and iterative prototoypes enable to fail in early stages and shape insights of the story the product is telling. I call this the UX-Conception & Prototyping pahse.

"I love, exploring new ways and possibilities of interacting with stuff to improove and redefine current mindsets to create joyfull and successful user experiences with meaningful user interfaces."

If the concept is bulletproof, the UI-Design begins. It defines how a hardware or software product looks, feels and behaves to the users input, responding on the users context and needs. From low-tech wearables over Mobile Devices and Desktop-Software to highend Industry Machinery and Embedded Devices.

Finally I guide and support the development of the product until it's alive to get a high quality final product.
